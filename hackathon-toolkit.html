Hackathon Toolkit

This hackathon kit is for people running hackathons independently from the Holo and Holochain organizations. Parts of the hackathon can be separate artifacts (e.g. ideation is a standalone piece). The overarching aim of this kit is to make hackathons we all run more effective by setting context, allowing pre-work, and allowing team membership to be interchangeable. The modules within this kit can be used and re-used in any way you imagine.

What's Next for Holochain Fans

Who is this for: This document is intended to help Holochain/Holo team members, external organizers, and facilitators explain to the newly acquainted what they can do to get involved in the Holochain and Holo projects as well as the Holochain ecosystem.

Note: Getting involved usually means participating in and directing others to our public chat channels. Please see the bottom of this guide for detailed instructions about how to begin.
