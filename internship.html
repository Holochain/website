Internship

Fall 2018

Apply Now!

The Summer 2018 Holochain Internship Program has been a smashing success! We’re so thrilled by the great work being done by our six developer interns, who have quickly learned how to develop apps using Holochain, and have made meaningful contributions to our documentation. Several of these interns will be joining the Holo Team to continue Holo app development, and all of them will continue to develop using Holochain with their student groups or other companies.

Because of the success of this program, we’re happy to announce that we’re opening applications for the next cohort of interns, who will participate in our internship program October 1 to December 8, 2018. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis and will close when we’ve selected our six participants.

Please note:

  • Application are open July 27 to September 15, and will be reviewed on a rolling basis, so get your application in asap.
  • Non-US based applicants must apply by August 10 to be considered.
  • HoloPorts: OS optimization, Remote Administration, Moble App Access, Containerization.
  • Applicants will be contacted after August 20th to schedule interviews.

Quick Details about the program:

  • The ten-week program requires 32-40 hours of Holochain development learning and practice per week (and more as you choose). You’ll practice agile-development tools and connect regularly (in person and online) with members of our development team.
  • Compensation includes flights to and from Denver, room & board during the program, and a small monthly stipend.
  • The internship will be in community, sharing household responsibilities like shopping, cleaning and cooking meals, and the process for sharing household chores will be co-created.
  • You are comfortable with: Agile coding, scrum, pair-coding, Kanban boards, etc.
  • You are comfortable providing and receiving direct feedback.

You should apply if:

  • You are, at minimum, a junior level developer.
  • You're familiar with distributed apps.
  • You understand full stack and are about building business applications / core apps for Holochain.
  • You've already downloaded holochain and have played with it.
  • You are looking to shift your career into holochain (for us or others).
  • You are available for an in-person residential training program in Denver, CO for the 10 weeks of our program.
